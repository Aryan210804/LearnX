    AI Masterclass

    Introduction to AI

    📌 What is AI?

    Artificial Intelligence is the simulation of human intelligence processes by machines.

    Narrow AI: Good at one thing (Chess, Alexa).

    General AI (AGI): Human-level intelligence (Theoretical).

    History of AI

    • 1950s: Turing Test.
    • 1997: Deep Blue beats Kasparov.
    • 2012: AlexNet (Deep Learning boom).
    • 2022: ChatGPT (Generative AI).

    AI vs ML vs DL

    graph TD AI[Artificial Intelligence] --> ML[Machine Learning] ML --> DL[Deep Learning] DL --> GenAI[Generative AI]

    Search Algorithms

    📌 A* Search

    Used in GPS navigators to find the shortest path.

    Game Theory (Minimax)

    Algorithm used in two-player games like Chess and Tic-Tac-Toe to minimize possible loss.

    NLP Basics

    Sentiment Analysis: Is this review positive or negative?

    Tokenization: Breaking text into words.

    Transformers (GPT)

    The architecture behind ChatGPT. Uses "Attention mechanisms" to understand context better than ever before.

    Computer Vision

    Teaching computers to "see".

    Pixels -> Edges -> Shapes -> Objects.

    Generative AI

    Creating new content (Images, Text, Audio) using models like GANs and Diffusion Models (MidJourney, DALL-E).

    Ethics & Bias

    Algorithmic Bias: If data is biased, the AI will be biased.

    Deepfakes: Misinformation.
