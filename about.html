About LearnX

LearnX is a premium, open-source educational platform developed as part of a mission to make learning programming simple, accessible, and structured.

Our platform mimics the clean, straightforward methodology of top-tier coding bootcamps, removing the noise and presenting you with purely practical, immediately usable knowledge.

🌟 What We Build

  • Web Basics: HTML, CSS, Javascript
  • Backend: Python, Java, C
  • Data & Analytics: SQL, NumPy, Pandas, Excel, R
  • Machine Learning: AI & Predictive models

The goal is to provide a comprehensive, structured digital notebook that anyone can reference while building real-world projects. No arbitrary paywalls, no confusing navigation — just pure code.
